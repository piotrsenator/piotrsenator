About Me

I work as an auditor, focusing on performance audits, strategic governance, and complex political issues. My interest lies in how public institutions function (and why they sometimes don’t).

I have an academic background in engineering and political science, which helps me think across systems and scales. I’m fascinated by the ways large structures succeed, falter, or quietly adapt.

In my free time, I walk: through mountains, cities, and ideas. It’s how I organise my thoughts, reconnect with what matters, and gain a wider perspective.
